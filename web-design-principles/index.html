The Castle of the Slave

Just a few kilometers outside of Amman, Jordan, there lies a beautifully preserved marvel of the ancient world. Qasr al-Abd, or the Castle of the Slave is a marvelously preserved palace from early in the second century. Come and explore this beautiful region of Iraq al-Amir in the wonderful kingdom of Jordan. Take time to invest in valuable historical and cultural experiences to enrich your life.
